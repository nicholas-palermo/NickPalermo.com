About Me

Hi! I'm Nick Palermo.

I'm a Junior Computer Science Student at the Macaulay Honors College at CUNY's College of Staten Island. I'm 20 years old, and I am an aspiring Software Developer. I never really knew anything about programming up until I got into college. I took my first Intro to Computer Science course as a 2nd semester freshman, and since then I've taken courses in Object-Oriented Programming, Software Engineering, Web Development, Database Systems, iOS development, and Digital Circuits.

I am fluent in many different techinologies in the field of software development including numerous languages such as C++, Java, JavaScript/HTML/CSS, PostgreSQL, Swift, PHP, and mySQL. Additionally I am familar with several frameworks and libraries including Node, Express, React, Angular, and Ionic, as well as several techonolgies including but not limited to: Visual Studio/VS Code, XCode, Eclipse, AWS, Git/GitHub, Unix Command Line, Netlify, Heroku, and MS Office Suite.

I have found that I love developing web and iOS applications. I think the simplicity of iOS design, as well as the clean user interface has inspired to create apps that seamlessly integrate within the Apple ecosystem. I look forward to working with new minds on fantastic new projects in the future!
